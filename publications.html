MPLP++: Fast, Parallel Dual Block-Coordinate Ascent for Dense Graphical Models

Siddharth Tourani, Alexander Shekhovtsov , Carsten Rother , Bogdan Savchynskyy

This work introduces a new MAP-solver, based on the popular Dual Block-Coordinate Ascent principle for dense graphical models. We verify the superiority of our algorithm on dense problems from publicly available benchmarks, that gives state-of-the-art performance amongst all message-passing algorithms for graphs with density greater than 10%.
